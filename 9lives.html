  9 Lives

üêàüÉè TableTop Game 

Overview:

"9 Lives" is a card game realized within the frame of the Global Game Jam 2021 in Mar del Plata city hosted by Globant.

9 lives is a cooperative card game in which we must arm ourselves to be able to make our way through hell to rescue our cat.


Components: 

  • 3 to 4 Players
  • Cooperative/Asymmetric
  • 45 - 60 min
  • 42 Demon Cards
  • 54 Object Cards
  • 10 Boss Cards
