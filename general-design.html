FEATURED PROJECT

'Phoenix Ikki' was my first attempt to a Character model. 
I've made this whole project for a contest and luckily I was one of the 5 finalists.
The prize was an open spot for the Master in '3D Modelling and Animation for Video games' in EVAD, Málaga. 

 Unfortunatelly I didn't win but I've definitely learned a lot!

PHOENIX IKKI - FINALIST 

PROCESS:

  • Model: Blender (based on human body references) 
  • Body: Skin modifier, Blender Sculpt tools. 
  • Armor: Solidify, modifiers with mirror, Lattice Modifiers. 
  • Back Phoenix Tails: Array modifiers with custom ends. 
  • Textured: Substance Painter 
You can visit the whole project in my Artstation
