Overview:

We embody a character half angel - half demon.
His mother was an angel and his father a devil‚Ä¶ for which he was unjustly deprived of heaven.
To regain your place in heaven, we'll come down to earth to fight demons trying to bring chaos.
The performance of our character will define its destiny.


It's a simple level of 2D platformer video game created in Construct 3 Free, a game engine that compiles logic-based events.

All assets used in the project are royalty-free, modified or purchased.

Global Game Jam

This game was realized within the frame of the Global Game Jam 2022 hosted by Globant in Mar del Plata, Argentina and I was collaborating from M√°laga, Spain.

Theme 2022

Duality was the theme for 2022, it could be represented any way you'd like it. Old/new, happy/sad, day/night, hot/cold, individual/team, peace/war.. etc.

Analysis of the Theme

We choosed to show the duality by using the character's background story between heaven and hell. Also using the good/bad moral concept.
