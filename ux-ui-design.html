DAM - HolstGym

Prototype realized for the 'App Mobile Design' Course by Escuela de Oficios (EOF) - Sevilla, Spain.  
Wireframe, flow diagram and mid fidelity mockup.
Tthe flow consisted in the "creation of a new account", going trough "login with social networks" to finally reach the "Onboarding" screen. In addition, modal messages were added for secondary actions such as "success or error notifications" and "login options with social networks".
The interactive wireframe, flow diagram and mid-fidelity mockup was built in Figma prototyping tool.

TimeOut - Technical test

The task consisted on taking an existing website and turning it to an App, focusing in usability and utility. Also presenting the flow of wireframes representing the main use of the app designing at least 3 screens. What is more, this technical challenge was realized in only 4 hours, going trough the architecture of the information in order to organize the process, prioritizing main screen and flows.

For this purpose I've used the Mid/Hight fidelity mockup technique with Figma prototyping tool.
